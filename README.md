# 🚖 Ola Ride Cancellation Analysis — Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes **ride cancellation trends** for Ola (Bangalore region) to identify key reasons behind cancellations and provide actionable insights to reduce cancellation rates and improve customer experience.

> ✅ **Result:** Implemented a new strategy that reduced the cancellation rate by **10%**.

---

## 🎯 Objective

- Identify the major reasons for ride cancellations by **customers** and **drivers**
- Analyze **booking trends** over time
- Understand **revenue patterns** by vehicle type and payment method
- Track **driver and customer ratings** across vehicle categories

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Microsoft Power BI** | Dashboard creation & data visualization |
| **SQL** | Data querying and transformation |
| **Microsoft Excel** | Raw data cleaning and processing |

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Overall Summary
- Total Bookings: **1K**
- Total Booking Value: **₹353K**
- Booking Status Breakdown:
  - ✅ Success: 63.9%
  - ❌ Canceled by Driver: 16%
  - 🔍 Driver Not Found: 10.1%
  - ❌ Canceled by Customer: 10%

### 2. Vehicle Type Analysis
| Vehicle | Total Booking Value | Success Booking Value | Avg. Distance |
|---------|-------------------|----------------------|---------------|
| Prime Sedan | 71K | 47K | 25.23 km |
| Prime SUV | 94K | 61K | 22.27 km |
| Prime Plus | 80K | 52K | 25.86 km |
| Mini | 87K | 46K | 24.29 km |
| Auto | 100K | 64K | 9.68 km |
| Bike | 55K | 38K | 24.27 km |
| E-Bike | 72K | 46K | 24.89 km |

### 3. Revenue Analysis
- Revenue breakdown by **Payment Method**: Cash, UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card
- Top 5 Customers by Booking Value identified
- Daily ride distance trends analyzed

### 4. Cancellation Analysis
**Canceled by Customer — Top Reasons:**
- Driver is not moving towards pickup (31%)
- Driver asked to cancel (22%)
- AC is not working (18%)
- Change of plans (18%)
- Wrong address (11%)

**Canceled by Driver — Top Reasons:**
- Personal & Car related issue (34.38%)
- Customer related issue (28.13%)
- Customer was coughing/sick (19.38%)
- More than permitted people (18.13%)

### 5. Ratings
- Average Driver Ratings by vehicle type: **3.98 – 4.07**
- Average Customer Ratings by vehicle type: **3.78 – 4.09**

---

## 📁 Dataset

- **Source:** Ola Bangalore Ride Data
- **Size:** 1,00,000 rows
- **Period:** July 2024
- **Key Columns:** Booking ID, Date, Vehicle Type, Booking Status, Cancellation Reason, Booking Value, Ride Distance, Driver Rating, Customer Rating

---

## 💡 Key Insights

1. **Auto** generates the highest booking value (100K) but covers the shortest distances
2. **Driver not moving towards pickup** is the #1 reason for customer cancellations — fixable with GPS tracking improvement
3. **Cash and UPI** are the dominant payment methods
4. Overall ratings are consistently above **4.0**, indicating good service quality
5. Ride volume shows **periodic peaks** suggesting demand spikes on certain days

---

## ✅ Recommendations

- Improve **real-time driver tracking** to reduce "driver not moving" cancellations
- Implement **driver penalty system** for avoidable cancellations
- Incentivize drivers during **peak demand periods**
- Focus on **Auto segment** for upselling due to high booking volume
